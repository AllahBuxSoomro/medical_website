The Medi Care Hospital is a not-for-profit academic medical center that provides comprehensive clinical services under one roof. In addition to its quaternary care hospital in Karachi, Medi Care Health System operates a network of four off-campus secondary care hospitals, 19 medical centers, and over 300 clinical laboratories in Pakistan. The system also includes 50+ pharmacies across 130 cities in Pakistan. Medi Care extends its care beyond hospital walls with Home Healthcare Services. Generous welfare and zakat programs are available to assist patients in financial need.

Recognised for its commitment to quality care and patient safety, Medi Care is accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI), a global gold standard in healthcare. Its Clinical Laboratories are also accredited by the College of American Pathologists (CAP), further underscoring Medi Care's commitment to excellence. In October 2024, the Medi Care Hospital, Pakistan, was honored with the 'Best Hospital of the Year' award, presented by the President of Pakistan at the FPCCI Achievement Awards.
